1 BET Multilayer Adsorption Theory

1.1 Introduction

We formalize the Brunauer-Emmett-Teller (BET) theory for multilayer gas adsorption on solid surfaces, deriving the BET equation which extends Langmuir’s single-layer model to multiple adsorption layers.

1.2 Physical Constants and Parameters

Definition 1.2.1 BET System Parameters
✓
#

Lean declarations

  • BET_parameters

A BET system is characterized by:

  • \(s_0\) – surface area of zeroth layer (positive)

  • \(E_1\) – activation energy for first layer adsorption

  • \(E_L\) – activation energy for subsequent layers

  • \(R\) – universal gas constant (positive)

  • \(T\) – absolute temperature (positive)

  • \(a_1, b_1, g\) – fitted constants (positive)

  • \(P\) – pressure (positive)

  • \(P_0\) – saturation pressure (positive)

  • \(V_0\) – monolayer volume (positive)

1.6 Auxiliary Mathematical Results

Lemma 1.6.1 Geometric Series Summability
✓
#

Lean declarations

  • geometric_series_summable

For \(|x| {\lt} 1\), the series \(\sum _{n=0}^{\infty } x^{n+1}\) is summable.

Proof ▼

This follows from the standard geometric series convergence test with ratio \(|x| {\lt} 1\).

Lemma 1.6.2 Weighted Geometric Series
✓
#

Uses

  • Lemma 1.6.1
  • Lemma 1.6.1

Lean declarations

  • weighted_geometric_series

For \(|x| {\lt} 1\):

\[ \sum _{n=0}^{\infty } (n+1) \cdot x^{n+1} = \frac{x}{(1-x)^2} \]
Proof ▼

This is the derivative of the geometric series formula. By differentiating \(\sum _{n=0}^{\infty } x^n = \frac{1}{1-x}\) term-by-term and adjusting indices, we obtain the result.

1.8 Main Mathematical Identity

Lemma 1.8.1 BET Main Mathematical Identity
✓
#

Uses

  • BET_main_math

For \(|x| {\lt} 1\) and \(C {\gt} 0\):

\[ \frac{C \sum _{i=0}^{\infty } (i+1) \cdot x^{i+1}}{1 + C \sum _{i=0}^{\infty } x^{i+1}} = \frac{C \cdot x}{(1-x)(1-x+Cx)} \]
Proof ▼

Substitute the explicit formulas from the weighted and simple geometric series lemmas, then simplify algebraically.

1.9 Main Theorems

Theorem 1.9.1 BET Derivation
#

Uses

  • Definition 1.7.1
  • Definition 1.7.2
  • Definition 1.3.1
  • Lemma 1.8.1
  • Definition 1.5.3
  • Definition 1.5.2
  • Definition 1.4.1

Lean declarations

  • BET_derivation

Under the conditions:

  • All physical parameters positive

  • \(P {\lt} P_0\) (pressure below saturation)

  • \(|x| {\lt} 1\) (convergence condition)

  • \(P_0 = 1/C_L\) (saturation pressure relation)

The general BET equation equals the simplified form:

\[ \text{BET} = \text{BET}_{\text{simplified}} \]
Proof ▼

Expand the definitions of \(V\) and \(A\), apply the geometric series formulas from the main mathematical identity, and simplify using the saturation pressure relation.
